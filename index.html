Privacy Policy

Void Blaster — Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Void Blaster is a single-player offline game. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Void Blaster does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.

Local Storage

Game progress (such as unlocked ships and completed levels) is saved locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Advertising

Void Blaster may display ads provided by third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob. These services may collect and use certain data to show relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Apart from advertising services described above, Void Blaster does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Void Blaster does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
